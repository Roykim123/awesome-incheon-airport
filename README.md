## Travel Tips
- **Peak parking hours**: Friday PM & Sunday PM (weekend travelers)
- **Best parking rates**: Off-airport parking services offer 40-60% savings vs official lots
- **Terminal tips**: T1 for most international, T2 for Korean Air/Delta/Air France alliance
- **Currency exchange**: Better rates at banks in Seoul than airport counters
